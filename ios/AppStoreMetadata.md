## Description

Transform your iPhone into a stunning vintage electromagnetic field detector. EMF Scope combines real magnetometer sensor data with a beautifully crafted retro analog meter interface inspired by classic 1950s scientific instruments.

**VINTAGE DESIGN**
Experience the charm of mid-century scientific equipment with our meticulously designed analog gauge featuring:
• Authentic brass screw accents
• Cream-colored meter face with blue arc scale
• Smooth, physics-based needle movement
• Green, yellow, and red safety zones

**REAL-TIME DETECTION**
EMF Scope uses your device's built-in magnetometer to measure electromagnetic fields in real-time. Watch the needle respond to:
• Electronic devices and appliances
• Power lines and electrical wiring
• Magnets and magnetic objects
• Metal objects and structures

**FEATURES**
• Multiple measurement units: MicroTesla (µT), MilliGauss (mG), and Gauss (G)
• One-touch calibration for accurate baseline readings
• Atmospheric Geiger-style sound effects
• Digital LCD readout display
• Light, dark, and system theme options
• Works offline - no internet required

**PERFECT FOR**
• Curious minds exploring electromagnetic fields
• Educational demonstrations
• Paranormal investigation enthusiasts
• Science and physics hobbyists
• Retro tech aesthetic lovers

Download EMF Scope and discover the invisible electromagnetic world around you through a beautifully nostalgic lens.

**Disclaimer:** EMF Scope is designed for educational and entertainment purposes only. It is not a certified EMF measurement device. Readings may vary based on device sensor quality and environmental factors. Do not use for safety-critical decisions.

## What's New (Version 2.0)

**Live Oscilloscope**
Watch your EMF readings come alive on a beautiful 1950s CRT-style oscilloscope. Scroll back through 30 seconds of history with a simple drag.

**Session Recording**
Record EMF readings with precise timestamps. Continue recording in the background for up to 3 hours.

**Session History**
Browse all your saved recordings with detailed statistics including min, max, and average values. Add notes to remember what you measured.

**CSV Export**
Export recordings to analyze in Excel, Google Sheets, or any data tool.

**Full-Screen View**
Tap the oscilloscope to expand it full screen alongside your session history.

Pro features available as a one-time $2.99 purchase. The classic EMF meter remains free forever.
